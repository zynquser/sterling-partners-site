Abdulla brings more than seventeen years of experience inside the Abu Dhabi Government and across complex cross-border legal matters for public- and private-sector clients in the region. He is recognised as a leading authority on UAE government practice and on wealth management and structuring for UHNWIs, family offices, and private holding entities — with the deep institutional knowledge of how Abu Dhabi's regulators, ministries, and government entities work. He holds qualifications from Paris Sorbonne University – Abu Dhabi and Harvard University, and previously served with the Abu Dhabi Judicial Department. As Managing Partner, he oversees the firm's wealth management, dispute resolution, and regulatory practice from its Abu Dhabi headquarters.
